How Rankings Work

RacketRank uses a sophisticated custom rating system to ensure fair and accurate player rankings. Here's everything you need to know.

The RacketRank Ranking System

Our ranking system is designed specifically for racket sports, taking into account match outcomes, opponent strength, and competitive balance. Every player earns their own unique ranking position that reflects their true skill level.

How Rankings Change

When you play a match, your ranking position changes based on:

  • The match outcome - Win, lose, or draw
  • Your opponent's ranking - Beating higher-ranked players improves your position more
  • The ranking difference - Larger gaps result in bigger position changes
  • Match importance - Tournament matches may carry additional weight

Example Scenario

Player A (Higher Ranked) vs Player B (Lower Ranked)

If Player A wins: Maintains or slightly improves position (expected outcome)
If Player B wins: Significant ranking improvement (upset victory)
