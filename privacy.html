1. Introduction

Welcome to RacketRank. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email, password (encrypted), name, age, phone number, username
  • Profile: Profile photo, tennis rating, match history, preferred language
  • Location: GPS coordinates, city, country (for matchmaking)
  • Communication: Chat messages, match proposals, scores

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • Device ID for push notifications
  • App usage patterns
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Matchmaking: Connect you with nearby tennis players
  • Communication: Enable chat between matched players
  • Rating System: Calculate and display your ELO rating
  • Notifications: Match updates, messages, score submissions
  • Service Improvement: Bug fixes, new features, performance

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

Public: Name, username, rating, statistics, approximate location

Private: Email, phone number, and exact GPS coordinates are NEVER shared

4.2 With Service Providers

  • Supabase: Backend and database hosting
  • Expo: Push notification delivery

5. Data Security

  • All data transmitted is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Strict access controls on servers
  • Regular security updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Profile deleted within 30 days
  • Match history anonymized
  • Chat messages deleted after 90 days

7. Your Rights

  • Access: View your data
  • Update: Edit your profile
  • Delete: Request account deletion
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications or location

8. Children's Privacy

RacketRank is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. International Users

  • GDPR (EU): Additional rights for European users
  • KVKK (Turkey): Compliance with Turkish data protection law
  • CCPA (California): Rights for California residents
