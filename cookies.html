Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 22, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how RacketRank uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website and mobile application.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and settings.
  • Authentication Cookies: These cookies keep you logged in to your account.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device until deleted or expired).

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies from our analytics and advertising partners. These cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these external services.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at support@racketrank.app.
