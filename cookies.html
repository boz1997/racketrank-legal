Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 22, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how RacketRank ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our mobile application.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for the app to function properly. These include authentication and security cookies. Session / 7 days
Performance Cookies Help us understand how users interact with our app to improve performance and user experience. 30 days
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and settings to provide a personalized experience. 90 days
Analytics Cookies Collect anonymous data about app usage to help us improve our services. 365 days

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in and maintain your session.
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and identify areas for improvement.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity and enhance security.
  • Performance: To optimize app performance and loading times.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics (or similar) to understand user behavior.
  • Authentication Services: Supabase for secure user authentication.
  • Error Tracking: Sentry for monitoring and fixing technical issues.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings.
  • App Settings: You can manage certain cookie preferences within the RacketRank app settings.
  • Opt-Out Tools: You can opt out of analytics cookies using tools provided by third-party services.

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the app and prevent you from using certain features.

6. Mobile App Tracking

Our mobile app may use device identifiers and similar technologies for analytics and personalization. You can control tracking through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at:
