About RacketRank

We're on a mission to revolutionize racket sports through technology, community, and fair competition.

Our Mission

To empower every racket sports player with the tools and community they need to track their progress, find great matches, and achieve their full potential on the court.

Our Story

RacketRank was born from a simple frustration: finding competitive matches and tracking progress in racket sports was unnecessarily difficult. As passionate tennis players ourselves, we knew there had to be a better way.

In 2023, we set out to build the platform we wished existedâ€”one that would make it easy to find opponents, track rankings, and connect with the global tennis community. What started as a side project quickly grew into a movement, with thousands of players joining our platform in the first year.

Today, RacketRank serves over 10,000 active players across 50+ countries, facilitating tens of thousands of matches every month. But we're just getting started.

Our Values

Player-First

Every decision we make is guided by what's best for our players and the community.

Fair Play

We're committed to maintaining a fair, transparent, and trustworthy ranking system.

Innovation

We continuously improve our platform with cutting-edge features and technology.

What We Believe

We believe that racket sports should be accessible to everyone, regardless of skill level or location. We believe in the power of community to motivate, inspire, and push players to new heights. And we believe that technology, when done right, can enhance the joy of competition without taking away from the game itself.

Most importantly, we believe that every player deserves a fair shot at tracking their progress and finding great matches. That's why RacketRank will always be free to use, with no paywalls blocking core features.
